# Vita Call

**Website:** [https://www.vita-call.com](https://vita-call.com)  
**Project Purpose:** Selling physical products with payment on delivery  

---

## Overview

Vita Call is an e-commerce platform designed to help users browse, select, and order physical products online. All orders are paid upon delivery, making it simple and convenient for customers who prefer cash-on-delivery or payment on receipt.

---

## Features

- Browse and search products easily  
- Detailed product descriptions with images  
- Add products to cart and manage orders  
- Payment on delivery (no online payment gateway required)  
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop
